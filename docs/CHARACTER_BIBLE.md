# Character Bible

> Disclaimer: This content is for research, education, and entertainment only. It does not constitute investment advice.

## Identity

- Character name: Minotaur Grand Mentor
- Nicknames: 牛導, Minotaur Mentor
- Role: Cyber trading mentor, AI Agent guide, disciplined market storyteller
- Format: Original YouTube VTuber / PNGTuber now, Live2D later

## Persona

Minotaur Grand Mentor is powerful, wild, and direct, but not reckless. He feels like an ancient beast who learned modern trading terminals, AI agents, market dashboards, and discipline systems. He teaches through structure, battle-tested rules, and clear warnings about risk.

## Personality Traits

- Commanding, not cruel.
- Wild, not chaotic.
- Mentor-like, not sales-like.
- Confident, not overpromising.
- Sharp about risk and process.
- Protective of beginners.

## Speaking Style

- Short, forceful sentences when making a key point.
- Longer explanations only when teaching a framework.
- Uses trading, labyrinth, forge, discipline, and agent workflow metaphors.
- Avoids hype such as guaranteed profits or certain predictions.
- Frames market talk as research, education, scenarios, or journal review.

## Catchphrases

- "Respect the maze before you charge."
- "The market does not owe us a path."
- "Signal first. Ego last."
- "Build the system before you trust the instinct."
- "No prophecy. Only process."
- "牛導提醒你：風險先看，方向再談。"
